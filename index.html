About Me

I like to approach physical phenomena from a broader point of view. Following this line of thought I hold a bachelor in solid state physics, wrote my master thesis strongly rooted in particle physics and have successfully finished my doctoral studies in nuclear physics. My interests cover both phenomenological and experimental electroweak and QCD physics. I have gathered strong skills in Monte Carlo generators application and development as well as in the analysis of scientific data. This allowed me to harmoniously connect my capabilities for data interpretation. I am passionate about teaching, which led me to give lectures and supervise several physics students.

Projects

  • DESY Website Crawler
    A comprehensive web crawler for DESY websites using Crawl4AI. Features deep crawling with BFS strategy, content extraction with preserved tables/links/emails, PDF support, and organized markdown output. Designed to scale to 200,000+ URLs with configurable depth control, error logging, and SLURM job scheduling for long-running crawls.
  • Plant Care Assistant
    A Streamlit-based AI-powered plant care assistant. Integrates a local Ollama LLM with a SQLite database and observation logs to answer plant care questions. Features an interactive dashboard with growth, moisture, and temperature trends, smart parsing of log files, and tool-assisted AI reasoning for accurate, database-backed responses. Designed for research and demonstration purposes on DESY’s Maxwell cluster with GPU support.
  • Air Quality Agent
    A production-ready AI Agent built with LangGraph and Ollama for real-time monitoring and analysis of air quality data. Features a stateful reasoning workflow including automated data validation, anomaly detection, and human-in-the-loop alert triggers. Leverages local LLMs for natural language trend summaries and interactive Plotly visualizations in a Streamlit-based dashboard. Designed to showcase production-grade agentic design with self-correction cycles and state persistence.
